Why I main Chrom over Roy

The reason that I main Chrom over Roy is the consistency and range that Chrom has over Roy. Roy is a stronger character when he hits his sweetspots, but that requires being extra close to the opponent to make sure they hit, which puts Roy at risk of being grabbed out of shield. Chrom however, can safely pressure from a distance using the whole length of his sword which is safer pressure on shield.
